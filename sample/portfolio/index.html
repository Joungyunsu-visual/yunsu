Joung-Yunsu observes how a person as an individual senses the world in modern civil society and focuses on the expandability of the sense of digital society. The artist asks about personal experience of the world through interactive art that interacts with the audience that "see themselves sensing", or through data visual artwork that accumulates the audience's experiences by data.

About

Joung Yunsu is a media artist based in Seoul, South Korea. Working at the intersection of digital technology and contemporary art, he creates immersive experiences through installation, performance, and audiovisual work.

His practice explores the poetic potential of data, light, and sound — transforming digital processes into sensory, human experiences.
